MiPhone

No more phone, no more problems.

See the process

Overview

How much time do we spend on our phone? Many people have no idea. These same people do not know how they could change their habits for the better: they have no data to look at, and so they don’t know how they can improve. By creating a phone application which tracks usage over time, we will allow these users to decrease their phone usage as they see fit, with goals they choose.
